## PHILOSOPHY SUMMARY

### What Actually Creates Fear (5 Mechanisms)
1. **Uncertainty** — The prediction gap. The brain fears what it cannot predict.
2. **Agency Removal** — Learned helplessness. Resistance is futile or makes things worse.
3. **Incongruity Detection** — Pattern breaks. The model of reality fails.
4. **Temporal Compression** — Imminent threat. Time collapses.
5. **Identity Threat** — Dissolution of self. The "I" is compromised.

### What Destroyed Modern Horror Monsters (4 Steps)
1. **Sympathy** — Monster given motivation/backstory. Understanding kills fear.
2. **Familiarity** — Repetition breeds recognition. Recognition breeds safety.
3. **Aestheticisation** — Monster becomes beautiful/cool/stylish.
4. **Metaphorisation** — Monster becomes symbol. Commentary replaces fear.

**Restoration strategy:** Reverse all four + make it personal.

### Arden Studios Signature (6 Pillars)
1. **Horror of Intimacy** — The threat is in your house, family, body.
2. **Horror of Understanding** — Knowledge is conversion, not protection.
3. **Horror of Partial Success** — Every solution makes things worse.
4. **Horror of Working-Class Pragmatism** — Ordinary people, extraordinary threat.
5. **Horror of the Ordinary** — The apocalypse happens where you live.
6. **Horror of Competence** — Smart choices, correct actions, still lose.

**Voice:** Understated, working-class, profane, funny, Australian.
**Rule:** Never explain too much. Answer one question, raise three.
**Identity:** Fear that begins when you close the book.
