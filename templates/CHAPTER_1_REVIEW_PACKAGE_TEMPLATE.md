## Provisional vs. Final Approval Note

External reviewers may grant **provisional approval** ("the structural work is correct, but I need to see the prose") before **final voice approval**. Respect this distinction. Do not proceed to full Book 1 architecture on provisional approval alone.

If the reviewer approves the chapter but requests a specific line change, apply the patch as a separate commit before proceeding. Do not bundle with later work.
